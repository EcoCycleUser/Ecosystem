About Ecocycle

We collect food waste from markets, restaurants, and households, and connect it with organizations that transform it into valuable resources. Our mission is to reduce food waste and promote sustainability through meaningful recycling.

How It Works

Food Waste Collected

We collect food waste from various sources including markets, restaurants, and households.

  • Daily collection schedules
  • Specialized containers
  • Real-time tracking

Verified for Processing

Our team verifies the quality and type of food waste to ensure it meets our standards.

  • Quality assessment
  • Waste categorization
  • Documentation

Supplied to Partners

Verified food waste is transported to our network of certified processing partners.

  • Efficient logistics
  • Temperature control
  • Secure transport

Transformed into Resources

Partners process the food waste into valuable resources like compost and biogas.

  • Compost production
  • Biogas generation
  • Resource recovery
